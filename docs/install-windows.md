# Installing WiiUDownloader on Windows
:::info Video Guide
<video src={require('@site/static/videos/WindowsGuide.mp4').default} controls preload style={{width: 100 + '%'}} playsinline autoPlay loop muted></video>   
:::
1.  Download `WiiUDownloader-Windows.zip` from the [latest release](https://github.com/Xpl0itU/WiiUDownloader/releases/latest).
    ![GitHub releases page](</screenshots/WiiUDownloader Windows.png>)
2.  Go to the download history on the top and wait for it to download
    ![DownloadWindows](</screenshots/Windows6.png>)
3.  Click the folder icon on the right side of "WiiUDownloader-Windows.zip" in the download history
    ![DownloadWindows2](</screenshots/WiiUDownloader Windows2.png>)
4.  Highlight "WiiUDownloader-Windows.zip", right click it and click extract, then, click extract on the bottom of the extract prompt.
    ![DownloadWindows3](</screenshots/WiiUDownloader3.png>)
    ![DownloadWindows4](</screenshots/WiiUDownloaderW4.png>)
5.  The folder for WiiUDownloader will open. Search for WiiUDownloader.exe then double click WiiUDownloader.exe to open it
    ![DownloadWindows5](</screenshots/W5.png>)

You have now completed the installation of WiiUDownloader on Windows. 

Continue to [Using on a real Wii U](./use-on-wiiu.md) or [Using on Cemu](./use-on-cemu.md)
